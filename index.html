The Berlin Compiler Meetup

The Berlin Compiler Meetup is an informal event focused on discussing compiler construction, programming language design, and related topics. It takes place about once per month.

The next meetup will take place either the 7th of the 14th of August.

The precise date will be picked in the course of this week.

We will meet at the Soundcloud office around 19:00.

Join the mailing list for announcements and discussion related to this meetup.
