The Berlin Compiler Meetup

The Berlin Compiler Meetup is an informal event focused on discussing compiler construction, programming language design, and related topics. It takes place about once per month.

The next meetup will take place around the middle of July.

We are still in the process of building this out. The date and venue for July's event have not yet been set.

Join the mailing list for announcements and discussion related to this meetup.

Talks for the next event

These are planned:

  • Alexis Sellier will talk about the way the Lua compiler works.
  • Marijn Haverbeke will present on the syntax and macro model of his toy language.

Did you study the internals of some compiler or VM? Are you working on a toy language? Any other war stories to share? Submit a talk! Simply write to the mailing list with your proposal. We'll schedule it for the next meeting.
