The Berlin Compiler Meetup

The Berlin Compiler Meetup is an informal event focused on discussing compiler construction, programming language design, and related topics. It takes place about once per month.

Next meetup: Tuesday February 19th.

We meet at the Soundcloud office [info] around 19:00.

At Rostenthaler Straße 13 there is a big entrance to a 'Hinterhof' with a restaurant in it. Inside this entrance, on the right, there is a doorbell with the SoundCloud cloud logo on it. If you press that, someone will let you in, and you can go up the stairs to the fourth floor, where the door on the right says 'txtr', and again has the SoundCloud logo on it. That should be open. If not, knock. We'll be in a room upstairs, next to the kitchen.

Talks for the next event

Did you study the internals of some compiler or VM? Are you working on a toy language? Read some mind-blowing papers on a related subject? Any other war stories to share? Submit a talk! Simply write to the mailing list with your proposal. We'll schedule it for the next meeting.
